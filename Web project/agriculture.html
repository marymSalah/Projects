AGRICULTURE


History


Eventhough Bahrain deals with a bad quality of soil and barely any rainfall considering its deserted climate.
It still historically was well known in its agriculture. In the early 1900s Bahrain was a main source of Date
production and exportation. Before the discovery of oil plantations, that was a big part of Bahrains Economy
While also being known as the land of a million Palm Trees





Types of Trees in Bahrain

Palm Trees were very popular in the early development of the island of Bahrain, it produced dates, which were deemed to be a vital aspect in the economy. Besides that, different types of trees grew on the island of Bahrain. All from citrus trees and alfalfa. In addition Bahrain is the home of the tree of life, or Shajarat-al-Hayat. It is a popular tourist destination and is over 400 years old.
